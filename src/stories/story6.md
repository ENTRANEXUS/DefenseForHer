A 35-year-old woman in West India has been arrested after her husband allegedly died following an incident in which she kicked him in the groin while defending herself from an alleged forced sexual assault.

The woman, identified as Rohini Kumar (name changed), told police that her husband, Arun Kumar (name changed), 45, who was reportedly HIV-positive, tried to force himself on her despite her refusal. She said she feared contracting the infection and reacted in self-defense.

According to police, the incident occurred around 2:30 a.m. on Sunday. Arun, who was allegedly under the influence of alcohol, woke his wife and demanded intimacy. When she resisted and he allegedly attempted to force himself on her, Rohini kicked him 9 times, causing him to collapse and die.
